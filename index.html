CHERRY BLOSSOM

OUR STORY

MOST DELICIOUS AND SATISFYING MEALS - Cherry
Blossom, at Castle Tower in Sydney, serves traditional and
modern Chinese cuisine to suit any occasion.

With a wide range of options to choose from, and an
extensive wine list, you are sure to find a delicious
meal to satisfy your appetite.

MENU

Come here with friends and family for some delicious meals. Cherry Blossom Chinese Restaurant has been blooming for many years now, with our professional and award-winning chefs, we have around 80 different varieties of steamed, deep fried, baked, sweet, savoury and spicy on the menu to choose from.

OUR SERVICES

Whether you're planning a grand wedding celebration or
a professional corporate function, our elegant function
rooms are the perfect setting for your event. Our
versatile spaces can accommodate any type of party or
celebration, from intimate gatherings to large receptions.
Additionally, our extensive food and beverage package
offerings are sure to impress your guests and make your
event unforgettable.
